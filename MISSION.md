# AIMS Mission

Build an open, modular, standards-driven platform that validates, explains, repairs, and preserves engineering data across CAD and GIS ecosystems while remaining vendor-neutral.

## Mission Objectives

1. Provide a stable internal model for engineering data.
2. Define open quality standards for CAD/GIS deliverables.
3. Validate engineering datasets with explainable rules.
4. Support reversible repair workflows.
5. Preserve validation, repair, and decision history.
6. Enable organizations to build custom profiles without changing the core.
